About us

"SIWY DYM" is the only restaurant in the area that can accommodate approx. 300 people people on two separate floors, a variety of ideas and architectural solutions will surprise even the most demanding. The whole interior is made of old wood obtained from demolition four hundred-year-old highlander cottages. Contractor and designer Józef Pintscher with the help of his son Grzegorz and son-in-law Piotr Majerczyk created a wonderful, homely and cozy atmosphere for possessions and fun on weekends with live music.

There are two floors in the restaurant. A large hall on the ground floor with a bar, a fireplace and a stage for music. On the first floor there is a large hall for 140 people with a bar, fireplace and mezzanine, and a stage for music.
